A11y Tokyo meetup soundboard

Time to say goodbye!

This is the A11y Tokyo meetup soundboard. It was created to give our visitors a gentle reminder to please leave the venue.

The soundboard is made up of 5 increasingly annoyed requests to please get out and one version of 夕焼け小焼け from Osaka. The sounds are:

  1. Thank you for coming, it is time to leave.
  2. It is indeed time to leave. Thank you.
  3. Please remove yourself from the premise.
  4. You can talk outside, too. Please leave.
  5. Fiery Red Sky
  6. The next song will be death metal. Please come again.
